    <h1>Research</h1>

    <p>
      My research focuses on offensive security in machine learning, with the goal of exposing vulnerabilities in AI systems before they are deployed at scale. I work at the intersection of adversarial ML, agentic AI security, and federated learning.
    </p>

    <h2>Publications</h2>

    <div class="project-card">
      <div class="project-title">Antitoxin: Stealthy Backdoor Control in Federated Learning</div>
      <div class="project-venue">SecureComm 2025</div>
      <p class="project-desc">
        A red-team framework for controlled insertion and removal of persistent backdoors in federated learning. Demonstrates evasion of modern aggregation defenses and challenges existing FL threat models. Achieves near-total backdoor eradication in 26 epochs across CIFAR-10, CIFAR-100, and EMNIST.
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="project-card">
      <div class="project-title">SMuF: State-Machine Mutational Fuzzing for IoT</div>
      <div class="project-venue">ICCIS 2018 (Springer)</div>
      <p class="project-desc">
        A mutation-based fuzzing framework leveraging state machine models and packet mutation to discover DoS, buffer overflow, and session hijacking vulnerabilities in IoT protocol implementations.
      </p>
    </div>

    <h2>Under Review</h2>

    <div class="project-card">
      <div class="project-title">Memory Poisoning and Privilege Escalation in Agentic AI Systems</div>
      <div class="project-venue">Top-tier security venue</div>
      <p class="project-desc">
        Novel memory-poisoning attacks against LLM-based agents with persistent memory, RAG, and tool use. Proposes SENTINEL, an architectural defense, evaluated across clinical EHR, question-answering, and e-commerce domains with multiple LLM backends.
      </p>
    </div>

    <div class="project-card">
      <div class="project-title">Text-to-Unlearn: A Cross-Modal Approach to Unlearning in GANs</div>
      <div class="project-venue">Security conference</div>
      <p class="project-desc">
        A framework enabling selective unlearning in GANs focused on human faces, using text prompts instead of annotated data. Supports expression and multi-attribute unlearning with automatic evaluation using image-text alignment metrics.
      </p>
    </div>
